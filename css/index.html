My Journey



Thailand is the travel hub of Southeast Asia. It’s the most visited country in the region – and with good reason! When you hear its name, you already think about nice beaches, elephants, Temples and great cheap food. And your thoughts are spot on. With its famed beaches, cool jungles, world-class diving spots, amazing food, night market, friendly and cheap prices, Thailand is one of the best country in Asia.

Last year, me and my partner went to Thailand to celebrate our Anniversary and I could say Thailand has a lot to offer. You can actually travel Thailand well without spending a lot of money because everything is convenient and easy. However, you should also be careful with some people when bargaining as they would always try to scam you so you need to be mindful of that.

Hong Kong has a lot to do. While you can visit the city within one or two days, it’s best to spend at least three days in Hong Kong. If you’re going to visit Macau, I’d add another day so you’d need 4-5 days to really see and explore the place. Hong Kong is also famous for incredible shopping opportunities, great food, magnificent harbour and of course, the Disneyland in Asia. It's very convenient to explore the city by having an Octopus Card and you can jump on a double-decker bus, tram, ferry or take the expansive MTR. It’s extremely easy to get around.

I went to Hong Kong and Macau back in June 2018 and it's my first and best international trip to remember. What's funny is that I went there during a strong typhoon but it didn't stop me from exploring the city. It was a great experience especially the side trip in Macau.
